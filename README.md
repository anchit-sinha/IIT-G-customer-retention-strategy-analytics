# Customer Value & Retention Strategy Analytics

## Business Problem

Can customer loyalty be driven by engagement rather than discounts?

This project analyzes customer behavior data to identify high-value customer segments, retention drivers, promotion dependence, and geographic growth opportunities.

---

## Project Overview

- Analyzed 3,900 customer records
- Tested 30+ business hypotheses
- Built customer scoring framework
- Developed Power BI executive dashboard
- Created strategic retention playbook

---

## Tech Stack

- Python
- SQL
- Power BI
- Excel

---

## Key Insights

- Customer Health predicts retention better than promotions.
- Future Champions represent the highest-growth segment.
- Loyalty is driven by engagement rather than discounts.
- High-value geographic markets offer stronger growth opportunities.
- Product category has limited impact on long-term loyalty.

## Strategic Recommendations

2. Reduce dependence on discount-led growth.
3. Expand in high-value markets.
4. Focus on customer health and engagement.
